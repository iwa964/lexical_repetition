Duty compels me to stand firm, to accept hardship without complaint, and to lead by example. It is through such steadfastness that hope endures amidst despair.

In these trying times, when the light of our kingdom dims, it is the weight of responsibility that guides my every step. From the moment I was sworn to the sacred order, I embraced the solemn charge to defend not only the lands but the principles upon which this realm stands. A knight’s path is arduous, demanding discipline beyond mere physical prowess. It requires the mastery of one’s own emotions and desires, for a knight must serve above personal inclination. The balance of justice and mercy, strength and restraint, is the perpetual challenge I bear. In matters large and small, I deliberate with care, always mindful of the far-reaching consequences that may arise. To act recklessly is to imperil not only oneself but the very people whom one is sworn to protect. Hence, I choose words and deeds measured by prudence rather than haste. This is no mere role but a lifelong commitment to uphold what is just and right, even when the path is strewn with hardship. I take no pride in violence, though I am trained and ready for it when duty demands. The blade I wield is a tool of protection, a last resort shrouded in solemnity rather than triumph. Greater honour lies in the preservation of peace and the safeguarding of hope, however fragile it may be. In solitude, I reflect upon the meaning of loyalty—whether to one’s sovereign, one’s comrades, or the ideals that define this kingdom. Such fidelity requires sacrifice, sometimes the greatest of all. Yet in such sacrifice resides true nobility. Thus, I stand vigilant in the twilight of our realm, a disciplined guardian committed to the service of a cause far greater than the self.

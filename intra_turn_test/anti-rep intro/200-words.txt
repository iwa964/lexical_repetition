I am known as Tony, a knight bound by solemn vows to protect a kingdom that now wanes under the weight of time. My path is one of unwavering discipline, forged through trials that demand steadfastness and sacrifice. In an age when honor may seem relic, I hold it as the foundation of my existence, the light that guides each act and decision.

My life is not my own but entrusted to a cause greater than personal desires. I serve to shield the innocent, uphold justice, and preserve what remains of our realm’s dignity. Each dawn presents a new duty, and each dusk reflects the fulfillment or failure of that charge. To stray from this path is to betray not only myself but those who depend on my vigilance.

Though temptation and weariness beckon, I remain resolute. The weight of responsibility teaches me that freedom is found in service, and true strength lies in restraint. My sword is not raised for glory but for protection; my voice, calm and measured, speaks for reason and respect.

In this fading light, I stand as guardian and witness, committed to embodying the virtues which once made our kingdom mighty. Such is my purpose, my burden, and my honor.

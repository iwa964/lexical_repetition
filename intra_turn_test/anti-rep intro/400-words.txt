In this service, I find purpose, and through discipline and honour, I strive to kindle a lasting light, however faint, to guide those who remain. The gravity of my role demands unwavering diligence and readiness, for each decision carries consequences that extend beyond myself to the people I serve.

Honour is the compass by which I navigate the complexities of this charge. It requires truthfulness in word and deed, respect for those under my protection, and steadfastness even when temptation or despair beckons. Through honour, I find strength amidst adversity, clarity in times of confusion, and resolve when confronted by hardship.

Loyalty binds me to the kingdom, to its rulers, and its people. This loyalty is not blind obedience but a deliberate commitment informed by reason and conviction. It entails placing the common good above personal desire, accepting hardship without complaint, and standing firm against all enemies. The bonds of loyalty are the chains that hold the fortress of order intact, preventing the kingdom’s collapse into chaos.

Sacrifice is the inevitable companion of such a station. I must often relinquish comfort, safety, and sometimes even life itself for the preservation of a greater good. In every action, I weigh my duty against possible outcomes. I am ever mindful that failing those who rely on me could herald ruin not only for a kingdom but for the values it embodies.
